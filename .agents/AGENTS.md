# Project Rules & Guidelines

## Brand Logo Integrity
* The **official brand logo** of Editroy is the **camera and scissors logo** (located at `public/icons/icon-192.png`, `public/icons/icon-512.png`, `public/icons/apple-touch-icon.png`, and `src/app/icon.png`).
* **DO NOT** use, introduce, or restore any other logos (such as the gradient "E" box).
* The browser favicon, dashboard sidebars, header navigation bars, and mobile app PWA icons must **exclusively** use the camera and scissors logo.
